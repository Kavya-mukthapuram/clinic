Our Story

Started as a single consulting room. Grown by word of mouth.

Wellspring Family Clinic opened in 2013 with one doctor, one room and a simple idea: patients stay healthier when their doctor actually knows them. Thirteen years later, that idea is still what runs the clinic.

Our Mission

Unhurried care, close to home.

We built Wellspring around a frustration a lot of families share — rushed appointments, unfamiliar doctors every visit, and having to repeat your medical history each time. So we set a rule early on: every patient gets a full consultation, and every returning patient is met by someone who has actually read their file.

That's meant staying deliberately small. We see fewer patients per day than a typical clinic so each visit gets the time it needs.

Our Values

What guides every visit.

Continuity

The same doctors see you visit after visit, so context never gets lost.

Clarity

Diagnoses and treatment plans explained in plain language, not jargon.

Accessibility

Same-day slots, teleconsultations, and transparent pricing — no surprises.

Prevention

We'd rather catch something early than treat it late — checkups matter.
